What is StreetHub?

StreetHub connects retailers and suppliers through group ordering for better deals.

How do I join a group order?

From the dashboard, select the item you need. If a group order exists, you’ll see a “Join Group” option. Click it and confirm your quantity.

How do I place a new order?

To place a new order, go to the dashboard, select your desired materials, choose quantity and supplier, then click “Place Order.”

Where can I track my orders?

Go to “My Orders” section from the sidebar or dashboard. You’ll see status updates like Pending, Shipped, or Delivered.

Can I compare prices of different suppliers?

Yes! When browsing items, use the “Compare” button next to each item. You’ll see supplier name, price, and delivery time.

How can I contact a supplier?

Click on the supplier name under the product listing. You’ll find their contact info or chat option available there.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept UPI, Net Banking, Debit/Credit Cards, and selected wallets like Paytm or PhonePe.
